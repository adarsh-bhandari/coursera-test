How to cook sandwich

Egg sandwich is my favourite breakfast. And I love it because it is delicious and easy to prepare. To make a delicious sandwich first collect these ingredients.

  • bread
  • egg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • sandwich maker

Now follow these steps

  1. smash the eggs in a bowl🥚
  2. whip the eggs🍳
  3. add salt and pepper🥨
  4. put the batter over bread🍞
  5. put the bread in the sandwich maker🥪
